## The Service

Submersion is a local-first dive logging application for scuba divers. It allows you to record dives, manage dive sites and gear, download data from dive computers, view statistics and dive profiles, and optionally link photos and back up your data using third-party services you choose to connect.

Submersion does not operate any remote servers and does not require you to create an account. All of your data is stored locally on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for details on how your data is handled.

## Dive Safety Disclaimer

**Submersion is a dive logging and record-keeping tool. It is not a dive computer, dive planner, or life-support device, and must not be relied upon for any purpose on which your safety depends.**

- Do not use the App to plan dives, calculate decompression obligations, monitor gas, or make any in-water safety decisions.
